Spiced Root Vegetable Soup

CHECK OUT IMAGE BELOW!

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • ‎
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
    • ‎
  • 2 sweet potatoes, chopped
    • ‎
  • 2 carrots, chopped
    • ‎
  • 2 parsnips, chopped
    • ‎
  • 1 red chilli, roughly chopped
    • ‎
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
    • ‎
  • 75g dried green lentils
    • ‎
  • 1.3l vegetable stock
    • ‎
  • 425ml milk
    • ‎
  • 100g Greek yogurt
    • ‎
  • 1 tbsp coriander leaves, chopped

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pan and fry the onions for 5 mins until softened.
    2. ‎
  2. Add the sweet potatoes, carrots, and parsnips and cook for another 5 mins.
    3. ‎
  3. Add the chilli and cumin for the final 2 mins of frying.
    4. ‎
  4. Pour in the lentils and vegetable stock, then bring to the boil.
    5. ‎
  5. Lower the heat and simmer for 25 mins until the vegetables are tender and lentils are soft.
    6. ‎
  6. Blitz the soup until smooth using a blender, adding the milk to reach your desired consistency.
    7. ‎
  7. Season with salt and pepper, then reheat until piping hot.
    8. ‎
  8. Ladle into bowls and serve with a dollop of Greek yogurt and a sprinkle of coriander.
