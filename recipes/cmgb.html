Chorizo & Mozzarella Gnocchi Bake

CHECK OUT AMAZING PICTURE BELOW

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 120g chorizo, diced
  • 2 x 400g cans chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tsp caster sugar
  • 600g fresh gnocchi
  • 125g mozzarella ball, cut into chunks
  • Small bunch of basil, torn
  • Green salad, to serve
‎

Steps

    ‎
  1. Heat the oil in a medium pan over medium heat and fry the onion and garlic for 8-10 mins until soft.
    2. ‎
  2. Add the chorizo and fry for 5 mins more until it releases its oils.
    3. ‎
  3. Tip in the tomatoes and sugar, season to taste, and bring to a simmer.
    4. ‎
  4. Add the gnocchi and cook in the sauce for 8 mins, stirring often, until soft.
    5. ‎
  5. Heat the grill to high.
    6. ‎
  6. Stir three-quarters of the mozzarella and most of the basil through the gnocchi.
    7. ‎
  7. Transfer the mixture into a baking dish or six ovenproof ramekins.
    8. ‎
  8. Top with the remaining mozzarella and grill for 3 mins until the cheese is melted and golden.
    9. ‎
  9. Scatter over the remaining basil and serve with a green salad.
