Charred Hispi Cabbage & Sausage Pasta

CHECK OUT PICTURE BELOW

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • ‎
  • 4 sausages, skins removed
    • ‎
  • 300g penne or short pasta
    • ‎
  • 1 small hispi cabbage, roughly chopped into small pieces
    • ‎
  • 1 garlic clove, finely sliced
    • ‎
  • 2 oregano sprigs, leaves picked
    • ‎
  • 100g crème fraîche
    • ‎
  • 1 tsp wholegrain mustard
    • ‎
  • 30g parmesan, finely grated
‎

Preparation

    ‎
  1. Heat a wide frying pan over medium heat and pour in the olive oil.
    2. ‎
  2. Squeeze the sausagemeat into the pan, crumbling it into small pieces with a spatula.
    3. ‎
  3. Cook the sausage for 5-6 mins until golden and charred, then remove with a slotted spoon.
    4. ‎
  4. Cook the pasta in a separate pan of boiling salted water according to pack instructions.
    5. ‎
  5. In the sausage pan, cook the cabbage for 6-7 mins over medium-high heat until charred in spots.
    6. ‎
  6. Stir the garlic and oregano into the cabbage, then return the cooked sausage to the pan.
    7. ‎
  7. Drain the pasta, reserving 2-3 ladlefuls of the cooking water.
    8. ‎
  8. Stir the crème fraîche, mustard, and reserved pasta water into the cabbage and sausage.
    9. ‎
  9. Add the pasta and most of the parmesan, mixing well to loosen the sauce.
    10. ‎
  10. Serve immediately with the remaining parmesan sprinkled on top.
